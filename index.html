About

Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell @ IESCE

Vision

To inculcate an innovation culture among the students, to create future entrepreneurs and position the institution, as a learning, innovation and entrepreneurial hub.
 

Mission

To establish an innovation platform, by introducing the state-of-the-art technologies, through promoting innovation and entrepreneurship.

Innovation

Innovation is the heartbeat of IES IEDC. We encourage each and every student, to come up with novel solutions to real world problems.

Development

Translating ideas from paper to ground is challenging. We provide an apt atmosphere to advance, channelize and develop students' ideas further.

Ventures

With our platform, students have an upperhand on networking and marketing. Thus helping them to launch brilliant startups.

Where there's a will, there's a way

Startups

The key to success is to start, period.

We provide a platform for students with a startup aptitude to grow and nurture their ideas, to the point of self sustenance.

Workshops

Learning never ends!

We strive to bring the best people from the industry to train students with cutting edge technologies. Upskilled education makes them more competitive for the upcoming industries.

Craft Allies

Elevating craft work

This is a social space where independent, talented and creative entrepreneurs exhibit their handmade projects. The motive is nurture the brilliance of our crafters and bring out their talents to explore better opportunities and showcasing their beautiful handmade art forms and projects.
